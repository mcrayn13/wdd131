Top 10 Biking Trails in the Philippines

From lush mountains to coastal paths, explore the most scenic and challenging biking trails the Philippines has to offer.

  1. Timberland Heights, Rizal

    Known as "The Bike Playground," Timberland offers trails for all levels, surrounded by breathtaking views.

  2. Mount Samat, Bataan

    Experience history and adventure as you climb the road to the historic Mount Samat Shrine.

  3. Blue Zone Trail, Davao

    This 25-kilometer trail offers diverse terrains and stunning views of Davaoâ€™s lush greenery.

  4. Kingfisher Trail, Cebu

    A must-try for mountain bikers, this trail combines technical challenges with panoramic views.

  5. Filinvest City Trails, Alabang

    A convenient urban escape offering well-maintained trails for both beginners and seasoned riders.

  6. Mayon Volcano Trail, Albay

    Ride along the base of this iconic volcano, with its picturesque landscapes and thrilling paths.

  7. Marilog District Trails, Davao

    Perfect for downhill and cross-country biking, with cool weather and scenic mountain views.

  8. Nuvali Dirt Weekend Trails, Laguna

    A popular destination for biking events, featuring varied terrain for all skill levels.

  9. La Mesa Nature Reserve, Quezon City

    Escape the city and enjoy a peaceful ride through this protected nature reserve.

  10. Itogon Trails, Benguet

    High-altitude trails offering challenging rides and stunning views of the Cordillera mountains.
